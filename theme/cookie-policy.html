Cookie Policy

Welcome to Fox Auto Services. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website, which helps us improve its performance and user experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: We may use marketing cookies to personalize the content and ads you see on our website and other websites.

3. Your Cookie Choices

You can control and manage cookies in various ways, including:

  • Browser Settings: You can configure your web browser to accept, reject, or delete cookies. Consult your browser's help menu for instructions.
  • Third-Party Tools: Some third-party tools and browser extensions allow you to manage cookies.
Please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last modified" date.
