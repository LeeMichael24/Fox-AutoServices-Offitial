Alignment and Balancing

Experience a smoother, safer ride with our expert Alignment and Balancing service at Fox Auto Services. Our skilled technicians ensure your vehicle's wheels are perfectly aligned and balanced for optimal performance.

Why is proper wheel alignment and balancing important?

  • Uneven Tire Wear: This can lead to the premature replacement of your tires, costing you more in the long run.
  • Reduced Fuel Efficiency: Misaligned or unbalanced wheels can increase rolling resistance, decreasing your gas mileage.
  • Poor Handling: Your vehicle may pull to one side, making it harder to control and potentially unsafe.
  • Increased Stress on Suspension: Misalignment and imbalance put extra strain on your suspension components, leading to costly repairs.
  • Uncomfortable Ride: A bumpy and shaky ride can make your daily commute a less enjoyable experience.
