Why is tire replacement crucial?

Your tires play a vital role in vehicle safety, handling, and fuel efficiency. Over time, tires wear down, leading to issues such as:

  • Reduced Grip: Worn tires can compromise your ability to maintain traction, especially on wet or slippery roads.
  • Handling Challenges: Handling and cornering capabilities may decline with worn-out tires, affecting vehicle stability.
  • Efficiency Drops: Worn tires can increase rolling resistance, resulting in reduced fuel efficiency.
  • Risk of Blowouts: Older and worn tires are more susceptible to blowouts, posing potential hazards on the road.
